Connecting Rural Patients to Doctors

Video Calls & Health Data Sharing for Affordable Healthcare

The Problem

  • Rural patients lack access to qualified doctors
  • High travel costs and time for consultations
  • Limited access to specialists
  • Poor digital literacy and connectivity

Our Solution

  • Lightweight mobile app for rural patients
  • Low-bandwidth video/audio consultations
  • Symptom and health data sharing
  • Local language support & voice input
  • Offline mode with auto-sync

Key Features

  1. Video/Audio Consultations
  2. Patient Health Data Sharing
  3. Local Language Support
  4. Offline Mode
  5. Doctor Dashboard

Tech Stack

Frontend: Flutter | Backend: Firebase | Video: WebRTC/Twilio | Database: Firestore | Extra: Google Translate API

Demo Flow

1. Patient logs in and enters symptoms
2. App sends health data to doctor
3. Doctor reviews & starts consultation
4. Patient receives prescription
5. Records saved for future use

Impact

  • Affordable healthcare for rural communities
  • Reduced travel costs and waiting times
  • Early diagnosis & better follow-ups
  • Increased healthcare inclusivity
  • Scalable for government and NGO adoption

Future Scope

  • AI-based symptom checker
  • Integration with wearable devices
  • Pharmacy and lab test integration
  • Government healthcare scheme support
  • Expansion to global low-resource settings
